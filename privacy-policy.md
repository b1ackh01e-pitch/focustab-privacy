# Privacy Policy for FocusTab  
**Last updated: June 04, 2025**  

FocusTab does not collect, store, or share any personal or user data. The extension creates mini-windows when you hover over links to preview content without opening new tabs. It uses the "storage" permission only to save your preferences (like window size or position) locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers or shared with third parties.
